  •   11. januar 2006 ca. kl. 20:25-

Trandal slekt:     Påkjørt av bil. AmerikaPer vilde gå over gata i Brooklyn, men vart kastet ca. 3 til 5 meter igjennom luftet i traffikken. Mirakelen var at, istadenfor å verte drept momentant, han vart nesten ikkje skadet i det heile tatt! Det ser ut som han fekk berre nokre små sår(?) ...Bilen kom i full fart råkjøyrande rundt hjørnet, rett inni fotgjenger overgangsfeltet.

    Trandal family:     Hit by a car. AmerikaPer wanted to cross the street in Brooklyn, but was thrown approx. 10 to 15 feet through the air in the traffic. The miracle was that, instead of being killed instantly, he was almost totally unhurt! It looks like he received only a few minor abrasions & lacerations(?) ...The car came speeding around the corner, right into the pedestrian crossing area.
