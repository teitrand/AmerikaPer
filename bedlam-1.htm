(The concept is somewhat similar to Jewish, Christian & Mohammedean beliefs.) Almost all Egyptians rejected his one-god concept that gave Egyptians the same God as slaves & foreign enemies. Pharaoh Ikhnaton destroyed temples & defaced old tombs, but in spite of persecution, the people worshipped their own gods in secret. After Ikhnaton ("the criminal") died, the people worshipped the old gods openly again.
