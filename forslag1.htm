NORD-DAKOTA "FLAPJACKS"   (oppskrift etter Bakke-Signe.)
North Dakota Flapjacks   (recipe by Signe from "Bakkene" farm.)

...Makes about 20 large pancakes for 5 hungry kids.

4 cups of Flour
1/2 cup of Sugar
2 rounded teaspoons of Baking powder
3 rounded teaspoons of Baking soda
1 teaspoon Salt

Mix together all dry ingredients, then add:
4 Eggs
1 teaspoon of Vanilla extract
4 cups of Buttermilk
1/4 pound melted Butter

Mix everything together. Beat only until smooth ...Batter loses air if over-beaten.

Fry on griddle or in a frying pan.

ONKEL PERS FISKEPANNEKAKER
Uncle Per's Fish Pancakes

NB-!! Bør lagast berre når stein edru.
            Note: Should only be made when stone sober.

1. Du bør helst være den strenge barnevakt til lille snille niesen din.
            You should preferably be your nice little niece's strict babysitter.
2. Ho må nekter å et opp alt fisken sin.
            She must refuse to eat up all of her fish.
3. Du truga: "Då får du den igjen til frukost!"
            You threaten: "Then you'll get it again for breakfast!"
4. Frukost: Ho halda fram å nekte å ete fisken sin.
            Breakfast: She continues refusing to eat her fish.
5. Du truga: "Då får du den igjen til middag!" ..men ho svarer, "Nei. Då skal eg ha' pannekaker! Det lovde du meg igår tidleg."
            You threaten: "Then you'll get it again for lunch!" ..but she answers, "No. Then I'm having pancakes! That's what you promised me early yesterday."
6. Middag:     Fiskepannekaker
            Lunch:     Fish Pancakes

        Laga pannekakerøra, som vanleg.
            Make pancake mix, as usual.
        Tilsetja kokt små opphakket fiskebiter.
            Add cooked chopped up tiny bits of fish.
        Overraskende bra!
            Surprisingly good!

Kan bruke syltetøy som fiskepannekake pålegg til frukost.
