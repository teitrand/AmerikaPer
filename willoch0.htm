Statsminister Kåre Isaachsen Willoch var født i 1928, og i 1951, berre 23 år gamalt, var han representant i Oslo bystyre. Berre få år seinare flyttet han til stortinget (1958). Han var statsministeren frå 1981 til 1986.

    Prime Minister Kåre Isaachsen Willoch was born in 1928, and in 1951, only 23 years old, he became a Representative in Oslo City Council. Only a few years later he moved to the National Parliament (1958). He was the Prime Minister from 1981 to 1986.


Følgjande bok, skrevet i ein øyra-på-skaft "eventyr" stil ("Snøkvit og dei sju dvergane") ..fortel om ein av dei mange kamp mellom Kåre Willoch (Høgre-fløy) og hans langtids politisk motstander Gro Haarlem Brundtland (Venstre-fløy) som også vart statsminister, omtrent same tiden Clinton kjempet for kvite huset.     [2005: - Brundtland har flyttet, og no bor i den varmt, solfylt, styrtrike byen Nice i Sør Frankrike. Ho sa: "Kulden i Noreg gjer kneane mine vondt."]

    The following book, written in a tongue-in-cheek "fairytale" style ("Snow-white and The Seven Dwarfs") ..tells about one of the many battles between Kåre Willoch (Right-Wing) and his long-time political rival Gro Haarlem Brundtland (Left-Wing) who also became Prime Minister of Norway, about the same time Clinton was battling for the White House.     [2005: - Brundtland has moved, and now lives in the warm, sunny, luxurious city of Nice in Southern France. She said: "The cold in Norway hurts my knees."]

    A bit ironic perhaps, ...Elisabeth Harlem, one of "The Angels" female back-up singers on the "Trandal's Song" CD recording is related to Brundtland.
